How the Game works

You start at a German Train Station. And have to find your destination. Your Destination is always an ICE (InterCity Express) Station.

The Game Provides you with the information on how long your journey is and how much transfers you have. Keep in mind that the time presented includes your transit and waiting time.

You know have to make your first guess by entering a valid ICE train station.
